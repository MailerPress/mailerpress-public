== Description ==

MailerPress is the ultimate email marketing solution for WordPress. Send email campaigns with ease, manage your contacts, and automate your email marketing efforts.

== Features ==

- Send email campaigns with ease
- Manage your contacts
- Automate your email marketing efforts
- Create beautiful email templates
- Schedule and send email campaigns
- Track email performance
- Integrate with your favorite WordPress plugins


Source available on our Github public repository: https://github.com/mailerpress/mailerpress-public

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/mailerpress` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
1. Use MailerPress screen to get started

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I get support? =

For FREE users: <a href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/mailerpress/support/">Forums</a>
For PRO users: support by mail from their customer account.

= Which data are sent to the ESP? =
MailerPress sends the following data to one of these email service provider (ESP) depending on your MailerPress settings to send emails:
- Brevo (https://www.brevo.com/legal/privacypolicy/):
  - Contacts
  - Senders
- Mailjet (https://www.mailjet.com/legal/privacy-policy/):
  - Contacts
  - Senders
- Mailchimp (https://mailchimp.com/legal/privacy/):
  - Contacts
  - Senders
